Sarvajanik Ganeshotsav, initiated by Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak in 1893, has left a lasting legacy. Similarly, our Adarshnagar Sarvajanik Ganeshotsav Mandal was founded in 1951, and this year marks the auspicious 73rd anniversary of our cherished tradition.

Adarshnagar Sarvajanik Ganeshotsav Mandal continues to celebrate this festival with the same fervor and enthusiasm as it has done every year. As we commemorate the 73rd year of this grand celebration, it brings us immense joy to connect with you through this website, specially launched for this occasion.

This public festival unites the entire Adarsh Nagar colony, comprising 68 buildings. Over the past 73 years, this festival has brought us all together without any distinctions, fostering a sense of unity and togetherness that is truly remarkable.
